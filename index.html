About Me

The person
behind the numbers

I'm Kuldeep Chaturvedi, Founder & CEO of JSK & JYMMA. My work sits at the intersection of business setup, accounting, tax, and long-term financial structure for entrepreneurs in the UAE and beyond.

I started my career inside the numbers—ledgers, reconciliations, compliance checks—but very quickly realised something: most entrepreneurs don't fail because of bad ideas. They struggle because of bad structure, unclear compliance, and reactive financial decisions. JSK & JYMMA was built to fix that.

My Journey
2012
First steps in accounting & compliance

I began working closely with SMEs, understanding how much time they lost trying to stay compliant while also trying to grow.

2016
JSK & JYMMA is founded

I launched JSK & JYMMA as a single, unified firm with one clear purpose: make accounting and compliance simple, reliable, and structured for growing businesses.

2019
Expansion into full business solutions

As our clients grew, their needs grew too. JSK & JYMMA evolved into a complete business solutions firm, adding business setup, tax, audit support, Golden Visa, and banking.

2020–2025
Scale, depth, and global reach

We've now supported 3,000+ businesses, built a 50+ member expert team, and assisted with global company structures across multiple jurisdictions, while staying deeply rooted in the UAE.
